Our Story

Born From
Obsession

It started with a trip to the highlands of Ethiopia in 2013. Marco stood in a coffee forest for the first time and realized he'd been drinking coffee completely wrong his entire life.

He spent the next year apprenticing with roasters across Colombia, Japan, and Scandinavia, obsessively learning each step of the journey from cherry to cup.

Brew & Co opened its doors in 2014 with a single mission: close the distance between the farmer and the first sip. Every origin we carry has a name, a face, and a story — and we'll happily tell you all of them.
